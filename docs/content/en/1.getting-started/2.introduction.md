Welcome to **Alamo Studio** documentation — your central place for installing, configuring, and maintaining our FiveM scripts.

## What you'll find here

Each script has its own section in the sidebar with dedicated guides for:

- **Overview** — what the script does and key features
- **Installation** — step-by-step setup for your server
- **Configuration** — every `config.lua` option explained
- **Troubleshooting** — common issues and fixes

## Supported frameworks

Our scripts are built to work with the most popular FiveM frameworks:
