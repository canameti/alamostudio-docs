Alamo Market is a framework-agnostic store system for **QBox**, **QBCore** and **ESX**. It ships with 20 ready-to-sell shop locations, a modern NUI, multi-language support and a fully theme-able color scheme.

#### What's inside

::card-group
  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-zap
  title: Plug & Play
  ---
  Drop it in, set your `Config.Locale`, restart. 20 LTD / Rob's Liquor / 24/7 shops are pre-configured and selling.
  :::

  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-boxes
  title: Framework Agnostic
  ---
  Auto-detects QBox, QBCore or ESX. No edits required to switch frameworks.
  :::

  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-palette
  title: Fully Theme-able
  ---
  Recolor the entire UI from one file — `variables.css`. Ships with Yellow, Green, Blue and Red presets.
  :::

  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-list
  title: Catalog System
  ---
  Define items once in a catalog, reuse it across many shops. Edit prices in one place.
  :::

  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-banknote
  title: Jobs & Payments
  ---
  Restrict shops to jobs/grades and control cash vs. card per shop.
  :::

  :::card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-languages
  title: Multi-language
  ---
  English, German and Turkish included. Add your own locale in minutes.
  :::
::
